about cheat day

cheat day is the new and improved grill and fry, with a new homely look and an updated menu. we believe that good food does not have to be at restaurant prices, which is why through months of trial and error, we have come up with new recipes and combinations of ingredients and fillings to achieve higher levels of taste.

placing orders through this website is still under development, so why not follow the links at the bottom of this page to order through deliveroo or justeat; your food is just a couple of clicks away.
